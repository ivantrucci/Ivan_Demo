134 + List of values for <b>PAYMENT_TYPE_CD</b> :
135  + Level Pay : Indicating the repayment of principal and interest of a loan at each payment period is a fixed amount
136  + Straight Line : Indicating the principal repayment of a loan at each payment period is a fixed amount
137  + Bullet : Indicating a lump sum of repayment of the principal at the maturity of the loan
138 
139 + List of values for <b>DAY_BASIS_CD</b> :
140  + 30/360 : Indicating every month has 30 days and each year has 360 days
141  + ACT/360: Indicating the actual number of days of a month is used for the month and 360 is used for the number of days in each year
142  + ACT/365: Indicating the actual number of days of a month is used for the month and 365 is used for the number of days in each year
143  + ACT/ACT: Indicating the actual number of days of a month is used for the month and the actual number of days of a year is used for the year
144 
145 + List of values for <b>INTEREST_RATE_TYPE</b> :
146  + Fixed : Indicating interest rate is fixed for the term of the loan
147  + Variable : Indicating interest rate is variable for the term of the loan
148  + ARM : Indicating the interest rate of a mortgage loan is adjustable over the term of the loan
149  + Hybrid : Indicating the interest rate of a loan is fixed for a defined period and adjustable for the rest of the loan term
